Background

Sanne St. Pierre is a Dutch-American graffiti artist, well known around the campus for her aloof, casual and often blunt personality. Absolutely unapologetic about her crimes, she's either a breath of fresh air or a toxic fixture within Avitus, depending on who you talk to. The existence of less-than-legal graffiti art littering the campus and nearby cityscape lends credence to her unique perspective towards life.

Background

Joseph is a capable young man, a skilled guitarist and a competent martial artist. Polite-yet-distant, he tends to interact however much is necessary before pulling back into his own thoughts. He has pride in his faith and beliefs, and a strong sense of justice and what is right. To those he considers a friend, he comes across as either composed and intelligent, or they lose track completely of what's going on in his head. Usually both.

Background

Natalie Doherty is an English writer and former kleptomaniac who is determined to atone for her past mistakes. Forced to grow up at far too young an age and raise her little sister herself, Natalie also wants to experience some of the fun that she missed out on as a child. Try as she might to break out of her shell, she can’t help doubting herself at times, making social interactions a bit awkward.
